REQUIREMENTS

  • R1. Data are in one of the selected smart city domains.

    Our dataset collects data of renewed water in the city of Madrid.

  • R2. Data are available as a CSV file.

    Our dataset is in CSV format in the folder.

  • R3. Data have an open license so they can be published.

    It has a license that allows the reuse of documents for commercial and non-commercial purposes.

  • R4. Data can be easily linked with generic real-world entities.

    Data can be easily related with other real life situations like drinking water supply for the population and irrigation of green areas

  • R5. Documentation exists for the data.

    The documentation can be found here: Documentation.

  • R6. Data can come from multiple data sources.

    The source of de data is "Dirección General de Gestión del Agua and Zonas Verdes" and this dataset collect information from differents locations as for example Viveros de la Villa, La China, La Gavia and Rejas.
