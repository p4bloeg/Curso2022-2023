Dataset Requirements

Pertenencia a el Smart City Domain(R1):

Los datos pertenencen a los distintos municipios de Madrid que cumplen con los requisitos de una Smart City:

- Tabular formats (i.e., SQL, XLS or CSV)

- Changing data (dynamic or streaming data), versioning, (automatic)
data quality assurance and reliability

- Legal aspects (e.g., licensing, data ownership)

- Access rights management or mechanisms for extracting public data
(plenty of confidential data)

- Data access through web services, proprietary APIs and data files
